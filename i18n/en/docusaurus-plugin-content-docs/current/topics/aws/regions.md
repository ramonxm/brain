# AWS Regions

AWS Regions are geographic areas that contain multiple isolated locations known as Availability Zones.  
They allow you to deploy applications closer to your users, improve latency, and meet compliance and data residency requirements.

## Why Regions Matter

- **Low latency:** Run workloads closer to end users.
- **High availability:** Distribute applications across multiple Availability Zones.
- **Compliance:** Store data in specific countries or regions when required by regulations.
- **Disaster recovery:** Design multi-region architectures for resilience.

When designing systems on AWS, always consider which Region best meets your technical, cost, and compliance needs.
